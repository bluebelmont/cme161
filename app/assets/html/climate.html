Visualizing our Climate Budget

This visualization shows global carbon emissions for the past 270 years and an estimate of how much more we can emit if we want to keep the earth within 2 degrees celcius of warming. The library used is C3. Data comes from OKFNUS and the Center for Internation Climate and Environmental Research. Based off of CME 161's C3 tutorial.

Flower Filtering

This visualization is an example of brushing and linking with DC.js and Crossfilter. Properties of flowers from the Iris Dataset are displayed and seperate charts, and filters imposed on any chart (created by clicking or dragging) affect all other charts. Based off of CME 161's crossfilter tutorial.

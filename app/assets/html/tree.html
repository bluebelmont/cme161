CME 161: Data Visualization Spring 2016

Tea Tree Visualization

This visualization represents the hierarchical orginazation of types of teas. Each node is collapsable by click, and the entire visualization adapts to the size of the window based on the as nodes open and close. The library used is d3.js. Based off of CME 161's d3 tutorial.

CME 161: Data Visualization Spring 2016

Boids in Three.js

This visualization is based off of repositories by wetmore, mrdood, and dderiso's CME 161 tutorial. The Boids algorithm was created by Craig Reynolds in 1986. It simulates flocking patterns of birds, which is an emergent behavior that arises from the simple rules of separation, alignment, and cohesion. In my rendition of this visualization, I've added the additional feature to have all members of a particular flock have the same color. This is also an emergent property, as the boids have only local rules regarding color assignment. The "color_rogueness" parameter adjusts the strength of tendency for each boid to consider themselves as a member of their nearest flock; the higher the value, the more likely the boids are to switch their color.

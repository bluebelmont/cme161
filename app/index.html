Tea Tree Visualization

This visualization represents the hierarchical orginazation of types of teas. Each node is collapsable by click, and the entire visualization adapts to the size of the window based on the as nodes open and close. The library used is d3.js.

Visualizing our Climate Budget

This visualization shows global carbon emissions for the past 270 years and an estimate of how much more we can emit if we want to keep the earth within 2 degrees celcius of warming. The library used is C3. Data comes from OKFNUS and the Center for Internation Climate and Environmental Research.

Flower Filtering
